# CareTel Services Overview

CareTel offers residential NBN plans, mobile SIM plans, and business internet solutions across Australia.

## NBN Plans
- Basic (25/10 Mbps) — $59/month
- Standard (50/20 Mbps) — $79/month
- Premium (100/40 Mbps) — $99/month

All NBN plans include unlimited data and no lock-in contracts.

## Mobile Plans
CareTel Mobile plans run on the Optus network, offering 5G coverage in metro areas.
