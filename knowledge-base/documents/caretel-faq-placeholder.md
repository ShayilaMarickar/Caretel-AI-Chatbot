# CareTel Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: How do I reset my router?**
A: Hold the reset button on the back of your router for 10 seconds until the lights flash. Wait 2 minutes for it to reconnect.

**Q: Can I pause my billing while traveling?**
A: Yes, contact support at least 3 business days before your travel dates to place your account on hold.

**Q: What do I do if my internet is down?**
A: Check the status light on your router first. If it's red, restart the router. If the issue persists after 5 minutes, contact support.
